The Name Behind the Brand

A name born from divine inspiration

ॐ

Radhana Art draws its name from the timeless love story of Radha and Krishna — a divine bond that represents beauty, devotion, creativity, and eternal connection. Just as Radha and Krishna's love is celebrated through art, music, and dance, we at Radhana Art celebrate your memories and milestones through the art of laser engraving. Each piece we create is infused with the same dedication and craftsmanship that honors this divine inspiration. Located in the heart of Kathmandu, Radhana Art has become synonymous with premium quality, artistic precision, and personalized service.

Our Journey

Our Story

2020

Founded in Kathmandu

Started with a simple vision — bring high-quality laser engraving to Nepal. A single machine, a small workshop, and a big dream.

2021

500+ Happy Customers

Word spread. From wooden QR codes to wedding invitations, we became the go-to laser engraving studio in Kathmandu.

2023

Expanded Product Range

Added neon signboards, 3D number plates, leaf art, and acrylic awards to our growing catalog of custom products.

2025

5,000+ Customers & Growing

Today, Radhana Art is one of the most trusted names in custom engraving in Nepal — serving individuals, businesses, and institutions.
